Support Our Educational Mission: Provide Free Learning Materials for All

Aya's Educational Worksheets started as a way to keep our daughter, Aya, learning during her downtime. We wanted to make her time productive and fun, and it turned out to be a great way for her to build skills and confidence. Seeing how much she enjoyed it and how much she learned, we felt inspired to share these worksheets with other families.

Again, we believe that education is a right, not a privilege. By offering these worksheets for free, we aim to help children gain access to educational materials, regardless of their circumstances - as long as they have internet connection.

However, maintaining the website and creating new content requires ongoing support. Your contributions will help us:

  • ✅ Continue providing free worksheets.
  • ✅ Expand and improve the platform.
  • ✅ Reach more children in need of educational tools.

Every contribution counts and helps us keep learning accessible for all children.
